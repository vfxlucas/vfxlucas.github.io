About Me

I’m a Compositor born in France and raised in Spain with 5+ years of experience in the VFX and animation industry. Over this time, I’ve contributed to feature films, high-end series and commercials, developing strong skills in CG integration, cleanup, and look development.

My background includes working in both large international studios and smaller teams, which has taught me to adapt quickly, collaborate effectively, and handle different pipelines and workflows depending on the project’s needs.

Most recently, I’ve been focused on expanding my experience with more complex CG-heavy shots and collaborating closely with supervisors and other departments, aiming to keep growing both technically and artistically within the industry.
